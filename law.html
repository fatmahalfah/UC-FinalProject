the Law definition

Law, in general, means that it is a science of the social and human sciences, as it is concerned with the study of the general rules and laws in the country, for which they regulate the lives of individuals who have to abide by these laws without discrimination. Thus, the "law" or in English "Law" is defined as the specialization whose main objectives are to defend the rights of people, the accused, and to prove their innocence. Students must realize that deciding to study law is not easy at all, and this major requires devoting a lot of time to study, analysis, deep thinking, studying real issues, and exposure to them, so that students can reach a deep understanding of their specialization and their tasks in the future; Students should think carefully before they enter the seats of law. The law major aims to prepare students legally in every field Legal areas such as: Conflict Resolution. Insurance contracts. International Commercial Arbitration

Advantages of law specialization

Law graduates get a lot of advantages, after they have put a lot of effort over the years to reach many advantages; Including the following: First: wide areas of the labor market, where the fields of work are expanding in the public and private sectors. Second: The human aspect of specialization, which includes justice, fairness, returning rights to their owners, defending people, in addition to proving the innocence of the accused

Disadvantages of specializing in law

First, the legal profession generally requires working long hours. Second: A graduate of this specialty cannot work without obtaining a long training period, to obtain experience and qualifications. Third: There is a permanent need and necessity for learning and reading

Number of years studying law

Studying the law specialization requires four academic years in the Arab world, in addition to a period of two years after graduation, in order for students to obtain a professional practice card, and this card is what enables students to engage in the labor market
