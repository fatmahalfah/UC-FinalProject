You must realize the type of skills that you must acquire and the qualities that must be possessed, before you decide on the choice of specialization and make the final decision about studying the literary specialization you desire

First: read! It is common that literary students need to train themselves to read continuously, and do not forget that a good reader is one who reads what is not written, takes notes, and draws many conclusions from the folds of the pages he read

Second: write! Students of literary majors spend most of their time in the art of expressing their thoughts and sayings, drawing and sculpting them in their imaginations before delivering them to people’s minds. They are responsible all the time for preparing and creating reports, and articles. Having a wonderful and distinctive writing style is the perfect step for creativity in the field

Third: critical and constructive thinking! Being a student in literary disciplines simply means that you can think and reflect deeply on everything you read, and what you touch on, and do not forget to look at the topics from all angles; One angle is not enough to open the way for critical and constructive thinking, and even unique creative thinking skills
