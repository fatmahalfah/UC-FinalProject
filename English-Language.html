English-Language definition

The "English Language" major is defined as focusing on providing students with the skills of analysis, criticism, thinking, writing, reading, and listening, in addition to producing texts in the English language. The English language major aims to make students fluent and creative in practicing the language

Number of years of study

The English language specialization contains 135 hours of study, and every 3 hours is equivalent to one subject. The specialization takes 3 to 4 years, depending on the number of hours and classes that students pass
