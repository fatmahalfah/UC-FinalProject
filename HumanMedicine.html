Human Medicine definition

The specialty of "human medicine" is defined as the specialty that means Diagnosing medical conditions and suggesting personalized treatment to her. In addition, it is the specialty that specializes in the prevention of Diseases by various medical methods and their treatment. And aims to specialize Human medicine to graduate doctors who care about health and wellness for the human being

Number of years studying medicine

Unlike other specialties, all medical specialties take seven years. Six of them are basic years, and the last year is called an "excellence year", and it is the year in which students specialize in a specific field
