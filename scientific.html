the future of Engineering

The demand for all branches of engineering is generally great in various parts of the world as it contributes to inventing many things in order to solve the problems facing man.

Although it is a globally sought-after discipline, it may be classified as saturated, or stagnant in some countries. Satisfaction is defined as the arrival of specialization to the point of sufficiency in the labor market in a country. Thus, it is difficult for its graduates to find a job.

Engineering in all its branches ranges from saturated to stagnant in Jordan, according to the Civil Service Commission. However, the demand for engineering specializations is increasing in the Gulf countries in general, as is the case in the State of Egypt, but the demand for the engineering specialization in architecture is high, and engineering specializations are required in European countries, especially civil engineering, and electrical engineering .
