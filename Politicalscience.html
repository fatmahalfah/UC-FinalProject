Political Science definition

Politics is one of the branches of social sciences or sociology or in English "Social Sciences/Sociology" that is concerned with the study and analysis of the laws and regulations followed by a country. Politics is the social science concerned with the analysis of all political matters of a country, including political beliefs, political activities, as well as political behaviour. In the past, people used politics to manage their affairs and establish rules and restrictions that control them
