Advantages of studying mechanical engineering


1. Acquisition of problem-solving skill. 2. The existence of a syndicate of engineers. 3. Specialization is required in the labor market. 4. Have a good income.

5. Mechanical engineers can benefit from their academic experience in their working life. For example, they can repair machines and electrical equipment at home based on theoretical and scientific knowledge .

3. The urgent need to follow the rules of security and safety and be careful Caution and caution in order to avoid the occurrence of danger.4. Additional efforts fall on the shoulders of engineers, which include reporting, supervision and evaluation processes, and attendance meetings, and more
